Demon Slayer

Natsuki Hanae, Zach Aguilar, and Akari Kitô

2019 - Ongoing
63 Episodes

A family is attacked by demons and only two members survive - Tanjiro and his sister Nezuko, who is turning into a demon slowly. Tanjiro sets out to become a demon slayer to avenge his family and cure his sister.

Genre: Drama, Action, Thriller, Animation, Horror, Family



Dannah   |  

December 2, 2024

Demon Slayer is incredible. The animation is smooth, and the fight scenes are breathtaking—honestly, it's like watching art come to life. Tanjiro is such a solid main character, and the bond between him and Nezuko is so heartwarming. The show does a great job of mixing intense action with emotional moments. The pacing is spot-on, and each arc feels like it’s building to something epic. If you're into anime that delivers both in terms of action and feels, this one's a must-watch.
