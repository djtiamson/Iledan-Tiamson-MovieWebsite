Our Top 5 TV Shows

  • By Dannah
  • By Sam

By Dannah


Running Man

Choi Bo Pil and Cheol Min

2010 - Ongoing

Running Man is a reality-variety show where the cast must complete missions at various places to win the race.

Run BTS!

BIGHIT

2015 - Ongoing

Run BTS (Dallyeola Bangtan!) is a South Korean show by the boy band BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan). The show is all about BTS doing activities, challenges, games and lots more.

Love Island UK

ITV Studios · Lifted Entertainment · Motion Content Group

2015

Single hopefuls looking for love complete tasks, couple off and get voted out week by week. Packed full of drama.

Drag Race Philippines

Arnel Natividad and Jennie Uy

2022 - Ongoing

The Filipino version of RuPaul's Drag Race where 12 contestants compete to be the next drag superstar.

Extreme Cheapskates

Christie McConnell

2011 - 2014

Exploration of people who go to remarkable lengths to save money for themselves and their families.


By Sam


Flower of Evil

Dir. Kim Cheol-gyoo, Yoon Jong-ho

2020

Although Baek Hee Sung is hiding a dark secret surrounding his true identity, he has established a happy family life. But his facade begins to crumble when his wife, Detective Cha Jiwon, begins investigating murders from 15 years ago.

The Devil Judge

Dir. Jung Kyu Choi

2021

During a time of chaos, a judge turns the court into something like a reality show and punishes evil people mercilessly.

My Liberation Notes

Dir. Kim Suk Yoon

2022

Yeom siblings, exhausted by the monotony of day-to-day adulthood, seek to find fulfillment and freedom from their humdrum lives.

Koisenu Futari

Erika Yoshida

2022

Sakiko never experienced romantic feelings in her life; one day, a supermarket clerk Takahashi tells her that there some people do not fall in love, and later she hears the word "aromantic asexual" for the first time.

Moving

Dir. Park In-je, Park Yoon-seo

2023

The quiet lives of three high school students with genetic superhuman abilities turn upside down when a mysterious organization starts to hunt them down one by one.
