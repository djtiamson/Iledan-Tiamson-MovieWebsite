By Dannah


Zootopia

Byron Howard, Rich Moore, and Jared Bush

2016

In a city of anthropomorphic animals, a rookie bunny cop and a cynical con artist fox must work together to uncover a conspiracy.

Finch

Miguel Sapochnik

2021

On a post-apocalyptic Earth, a robot, built to protect the life of his creator's beloved dog learns about life, love, friendship and what it means to be human.

The How's Of Us

Cathy Garcia-Sampana

2018

A young couple dreams of growing old together as they as they deal with the struggles of being in a long-term relationship.

Vivarium

Lorcan Finnegan

2019

A young couple looking for the perfect home find themselves trapped in a mysterious labyrinth-like neighborhood of identical houses.

The Platform

Galder Gaztelu-Urrutia

2019 and 2024

In a prison where inmates are fed on a descending platform, those on the upper levels take more than their fair share while those below are left to starve on scraps and one man decides to change the system.


By Sam


One for the Road

Dir. Baz Poonpiriya

2021

Boss comes back to Thailand to help his best friend Aood accomplish his final bucket list. Aood has an unusual last mission for someone who has terminal cancer: to meet his ex-girlfriends to give something back.

Decision to Leave

Dir. Park Chan-wook

2022

Detective Jang Hae-joon is investigating the death of a man in the mountains when he encounters the man's mysterious wife, Song Seo-rae, during his relentless sleuthing.

Everything Everywhere All at Once

Dir. Daniel Kwan, Daniel Scheinert

2021

Evelyn is caught up in a wild adventure where she must save existence by exploring alternate universes and connecting with the lives she could have led.

The Paradise of Thorns

Dir. Boss Kuno

2024

Thongkam and Sek, an unmarried gay couple, worked tirelessly to build a home and durian farm together. After Sek's accidental death, his family seizes assets due to the lack of legal marriage recognition which leaves Thongkam with nothing. Thongkam fights to reclaim their shared property.

Lahn Mah

Dir. Pat Boonnitipat

2024

M quits work to care for his dying grandmother, Amah, motivated by her fortune. He schemes to win her favor before she passes.
